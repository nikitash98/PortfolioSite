LV JASPERS

A photorealistic pair of game-ready, VR-capable Louis Vuitton X Kanye Jaspers. These were modeled and textured for a freelance client for use in VRChat. The poster was designed in Illustrator.

Textures were either generated from photographic reference, or procedurally with Substance Designer.

Tools used: Zbrush, Maya, Arnold, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, Photoshop

Quantum Jam: Nerf Exploration

My contribution on a team for the IBM + Touchdesigner Quantum Design Jam challenge. I used the NERF process, and set up NVIDIA's codebase to generate ephemeral, painterly scenes. The concept was to use quantum information to allow the computer to decide whether to display. I used both rendered video from within the NERF application, and the NERF 3D information to create a novel way to transition between scenes.

MR. POP
Mr. Pop is my winning entry for the Polycount Quarterly Character Art Challenge.

He was modeled based on a fantastic piece of concept art by Gaston Garcia. I wanted to follow the sillhouette of the concept, but push the realism of the character in the texturing and detailing. The sugar flakes were procedurally generated using Maya's MASH tools.

Tools used:
Zbrush, Maya, Arnold, Substance Painter, Photoshop

ANATOMY OF A GUY
A (meant to be funny) poster of one of my anatomy studies. I wanted to inject some humor into a usually dreary subject.

I sculpted the ecorche in Zbrush as a full study of the human figure. I then posed and rendered it. Finally, I brought it into Illustrator and designed the poster around it.

Tools used: Illustrator, Zbrush, Arnold, Maya

KULIPARI POSTERS
A pair of posters designed at OVFX for distribution and large scale printing at Kidscreen and Comic Con.

I posed, lit, and rendered the characters. Then I used photobashing and stock images to fill out the images. This saved the team time from having to model and render setting and accessories, while also allowing more on-the-fly changes to the composition.

Tools used: Maya, Arnold, Photoshop, Illustrator
